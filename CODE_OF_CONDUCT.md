# Code of Conduct

Please participate respectfully and constructively.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions and feedback
- Assume good intent and keep criticism specific
- Focus on improving the project
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Abusive or hostile language
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration harder

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the repository owner privately through GitHub.
